Text Summarizer
Step 1. Get some text
Step 2. Use stemmer and remove stopwords
Step 3. Apply word tokenization
Step 4. Make a frequency table which contains count of every word
Step 5. Apply Sentence tokenizer
Step 6. Now for every word count the frequency of word in each sentence 
Step 7. Calculate sum of all frequencies that was calculated in step 6
Step 8. Calculate Average
Step 9. Now compare sentence value greater than some value like 1.2 and multiply with average
